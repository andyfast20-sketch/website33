Instead of figuring out which script to run, just use the **MENU**!

### How to Use:

1. **Double-click:** `MENU.bat`
2. **Choose what you want to do:**

   - Press `2` to **Create Package** (for another server)

   - Press `5` to **Configure** (edit API keys)
   - Press `6` to **Help** (view guides)
   - Press `7` to **Status** (check what's installed)
   - Press `8` to **Exit**
